Support

Artwork Retraction

 

Sometimes a community poster might wish to have their artwork retracted from the gallery. We understand that this can sometimes be the case with artists such as when moving to another platform or due to an imperfection.

 

Before submitting a retraction request to Cherry Universe please make sure that you really wish to do this, Artwork can be re-submitted but may rank lower in the gallery or might loose its long-time standing.

 

Please also note, Artwork retraction requests can be denied if the reason is insufficient as was agreed when you as the artist submitted your work to the "Gallery"

 

Fill out the following google form to request a retraction: https://forms.gle/6pfdP4nHz1Qb8MTM8

Low quality image / offensive art?

 

In the event that we posted artwork that may be too low in quality or may be offensive to you. We are happy to provide support by reviewing and taking action against the issue reported.

 

Unlike with the artwork retraction offensive art may be edited by us or removed without warning. Low quality images will be fixed or a notice will be posted if quality cannot be repaired.

 

To submit a issue for this please email us at celestial@cherryuniverse.co.uk or contact support on Solaris Chat through discord.
