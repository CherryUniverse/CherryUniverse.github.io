Shaded Images!

 

Meet the images of the Cherry City world taken on the 14th January 2021 after converting the world to Minecraft Java and using shaders to capture the world.

 

During the photo's of these worlds Cherry City had been closed for nearly a year but was on its way back to merging again with the Oaktree Universe transition.

 

Cherry City and Oaktree later went on to merge into one Universe featuring both of the worlds where progression continued in both of the worlds as they were before.

Not as Glamorous

 

While the shader versions of the Cherry City world images are much better and nicer to look at they show a modified and not true original version of the game. Such shader image variants have not been in existence since.

 

These images are the true final world images and were taken in the final hour that Cherry City had a active world status. Shortly after these images were taken the world was announced dead and Oaktree City took the main stage.

 

These images have no shaders and better represent all the Oaktree Images that are available.
