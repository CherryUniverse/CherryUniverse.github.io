Universal Behaviour

A small modification pack designed to make the mobs that you do not want or need to not spawn on easy or normal. Universal Behaviour allows for a Survival mode Minecraft with hunger to be played like it is in normal mode.

 

The best pack for role-play and even general survival!

Open for modification!

Using a semi open license Universal Behaviour allows for the modification and editing of  the pack for own or commercial use. Distribution of the pack is allowed if linked back to the original pack.
