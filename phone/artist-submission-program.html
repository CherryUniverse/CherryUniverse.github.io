Artist Submission Program

Interested to get your work on The CU Gallery?

 

Here you can find information on what you agree to when you submit artwork to "The Gallery" and how your artwork will be processed and used within the CU globe. It is important that you read through everything on this page before submitting your work to Cherry Universe!

Artist Submission Program - Terms & Conditions

 

When submitting artwork to the Cherry Universe artist submission program you are to state your name or alias that you wish to use and submit a title for your work. When you do this the gallery will make sure that your work is captioned with the title and your name or alias that you have chosen to use. When you submit your work to us you agree that Cherry Universe may post your artwork across the CU service globe and be able to make minor modifications to your work such as fixing colour issues, trimming or cropping of the image. CU will also be able to take and use modified or unmodified said artwork throughout the entire service globe and is not limited to just "The Gallery". When you submit your work to Cherry Universe you agree that "Cherry Universe LLC" will have unlimited and never ending access to be able to post, publish and use your artwork as it sees fit as long as credit is given to the artist. Cherry Universe cannot however profit from or sell artwork that has been modified by Cherry Universe. By submitting you agree that Cherry Universe will be allowed to have access to your submission and use it whenever it pleases in guidance with previously stated terms. Artists will not be able to revoke access to artwork once submitted unless it is lewd or contains stolen property in which artists that have submitted will need to file a claim to have the artwork removed. Cherry Universe will not be liable for any legal prosecution due to artists submitting work that violates any UK copyright or licensing law and that the Submitting artist will be open for legal repercussions. Once artwork has been submitted to Cherry Universe artists may suggest that another version be used due to quality or legal reasons although Cherry Universe reserves the right to continue usage of the older copy of artwork with or against the wishes of the artist. When submitting work artists are not limited to just posting on Cherry Universe and are in no way legally locked to using just Cherry Universe as a means of recognition. Artists may however not enter a exclusive contract with another distributor prior to submitting the artwork to the Cherry Universe gallery, if an artist does this they may be liable to a breach of contract fee depending on the cost for covering all legal fees caused by the artists actions. Cherry Universe cannot be held accountable for breaching of any contract retaining to the artwork and all legal issues are the responsibility of the submitting artist. Cherry Universe must also inform submitting artists of any new terms and conditions changes giving the artist the ability to deny the new terms and conditions but Cherry Universe will still be able to continue using artist's artwork utilizing the previous terms and conditions contract. When artists sign up for this contract and submit their artwork they agree that the length of the contract will not end and that all conditions mentioned within the terms and conditions section are agreed upon.

How to submit

 

Submission is easy, when an artist has their work ready for submission they may submit their artwork one of two ways.

 

They may send their artwork to Cherry Universe through a social media platform or Email cherry universe at celestial@cherryuniverse.co.uk

 

When an artist submits their work to Cherry Universe they agree to the current Terms and Conditions and that they format their submission with a Their name or Alias that they wish to use and a title for their artwork that will be used.

 

Artists are allowed to change their reference name through submitting the name change to celestial@cherryuniverse.co.uk
