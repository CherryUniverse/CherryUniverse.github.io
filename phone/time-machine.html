Cherry Universe

 

The version history of Cherry Universe, from V1 to yesterdays version. Experienced just as it was when the version was last online!

Cherry Universe V1

 

The first ever release of CU and the first version to ever be launched.

Cherry Universe V2

 

Minor improvements with top bar nav and a brand new star logo.

Cherry Universe V3

 

The final of the original era and the only was minor changes.

Cherry Universe V4

 

The first version of the Continuity UI and the largest redesign to CU yet.

Cherry Universe V5

 

Continuity Updates, News expansion and more V5 was a upgrade to 4.

Cherry Universe V6

 

The final stardot continuity UI and the final of the old CU design era.

Cherry Universe V7

 

New Logo, Smooth Gradients an a new design. V7 brought major changes.

Cherry Universe V8

 

Data-reports, Peak continuity and packed of features. V8 was the peak of the old era.

Cherry Universe V9

 

Last of Continuity, messaged games and the end of "The gallery" and an era.

Cherry Universe V10

 

Framed Icons, Click-able navigation and a square design. The new era!

Cherry Universe V11

 

Updated Visuals, Square Hybrid and minor changes. Gone was squares!

Cherry Universe V12

 

The all new Design era, Generational changes and a new logo.

Cherry Universe V13

 

Moon Design, New wallpapers and a weird time in the design of CU.

Cherry Universe V14

 

Short lived hub design, Final Release with background images.

Cherry Universe V15

 

Improved User Experience, Gradients again and more user friendly.

Cherry Universe V16

 

A new logo and a awful UI. Function took over form and was a dark time.

Cherry Universe V17

 

Graphically impressive, Sleek and modern web design With new icons and more.

Cherry Universe V18

 

The final true classic design and the longest supported CU classic Version.

Cherry Universe V19

 

Transitioning, Moving and iffy, V19 was a port for old to new as V20 dawned.

Cherry Service Globe

 

Classic and Discontinued Cherry Universe Services external websites, From Cherry City to Copen Server these are the websites that CU Ditched.

Cherry City Web

 

Cherry City Web, The first of the Cherry Universe websites and one of the oldest ran, 2019-2022 for the Cherry City Minecraft Server!

Oaktree Universe Web

 

Oaktree Universe Web, From 2020 - 2022 Oaktree Universe Web introduced U-NET and at a time was integrated within CU itself.

Copen Server Web

 

The website of the short run Copen Server, 2022-2022. Not much contained as Copen Server was not a large world.
