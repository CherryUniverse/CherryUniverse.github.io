Welcome to Universal Minecraft

Universal Minecraft is the mobile phone soloution to downloading and using our Bedrock packs on mobile devices!

Cherry City

 

Download the first of the Cherry Universe Minecraft server trio. Ran between 2019 and 2020 Cherry City was at the time one of the biggest Minecraft worlds that we have ever made and today stands at 2nd place!

Oaktree Universe

 

Oaktree Universe, Download the world through the CNP HTTP archive and explore Oaktree through the ages from 2020 to 2022 during the runtime of the largest Cherry Universe MC World we have ever made!

Cherry Universe Bedrock Packs

From the early days of Cherry City to the final day of Copen Server Cherry Universe has produced many different high quality packs. From the Universal Suite to Cherry Rebuild meet the packs with 0 added extra BS to them.

Cherry Rebuild 2023

Downloading through MCPEDL, Cherry Rebuild 2023 is the Minecraft UI enhancement pack that is simple and elegant.

Universal Behaviour

A small Bedrock pack designed to make mobs that you do not want in your world to spawn on hard mode while keeping the game fully playable. Including a Semi Open license for modification!

Universal Seasons

A suite of packs designed to theme your world as the season, From Autumn to spring we have 3/4 seasons all having 0 extra BS onto the pack and designed to work closely with Universal Plugins!
