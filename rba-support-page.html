How to get Support

 

To access support for the Rebuild Archive you have 2 options. Users may contact staff through Solaris Chat and ask for support there.
For Unsupported versions users may be able to get support from other users or some staff through the Forums on Solaris Chat as well.

 

For other queries users may be able to contact us through email at celestial@cherryuniverse.co.uk
