Welcome to Oaktree Universe

 

Oaktree universe was a Minecraft Server / XBOX Live hosted server that ran from 2020 to 2022.

Being the predecessor to the Cherry City Minecraft world and building upon the creations of the previous server world Oaktree introduced and ushered in a whole new generation of server.


From new ways of running in a survival only world to little command block influence Oaktree made the world fair game and brought one of the best ways from running Minecraft that we have ever had.

 

Oaktree also ushered in the Pack and Mod era with the introduction of many mods and modpacks into the game some own brand and some made externally.


Oaktree was the most advanced world yet.

Oaktree's History!

 

Launching 18th Febuary 2020 Oaktree overlapped the end date of Cherry City by 1 month meaning both the worlds ran side by side, it was made originally to be a separate survival world between me and Conagh but ended up evolving into the replacement for the dying Cherry City.

 

Introducing a new Management style also meant the world could not be commanded, Meaning that when it came to it, the world evolved over time and history was preserved and with the lack of creative mode the Server felt dynamic and fluid.

 

Oaktree is also well known for introducing the Minecraft Pack revolution with innovations like Cherry Rebuild, Universal Behaviour and even the Universal Seasons pack, Oaktree was one of the most advanced worlds that we had made to date.
