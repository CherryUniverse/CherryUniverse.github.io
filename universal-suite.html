Universal Suite

Universally Clean & Simple!

Universal Season's

 

Welcome to Universal Season's, A clean and simple designed Minecraft Pack suite designed to theme the Minecraft World without adding all the extra stuff which other packs do!

Universal Devices

 

Meet Universal Devices, The pack designed to introduce many different portable tech devices into Minecraft.

 

From phones to ipad's Universal Devices is one of the cleanest and best pack's for adding electronic tech into your Minecraft World!

Universal Behaviour

 

Universal Behaviour, Its a simple pack used by all of the Cherry Universe Minecraft worlds removing different Minecraft mobs from normal and easy mode to create a peaceful hunger using and completable version of Minecraft.

 

Following the same simple design of other Universal Packs!

Universal Plugin's

 

Meet the Universal Plug-ins, Small tiny pack plug-ins that add extra functionality and features to the base universal pack.

 

Able to work with and without the base Universal Packs, Plugins are designed to bring options to the barren and base Universal Packs.
