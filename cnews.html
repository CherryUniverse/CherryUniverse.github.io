Welcome to The Newsroom

 

The Newsroom is a information global hub for Cherry Universe users where press releases will be issued, Update information and much more for users to view.

 

There are 2 separate sections of Newsroom, News Release and Classic Articles.

News Release is the new format featuring faster paced news information, better formatting and a much more inclusive news reporting style such as press releases and more information like that.

Classic Articles is the traditional C-NEWS. Where news articles are written and published and more traditional slower paced news is made available to the public.
