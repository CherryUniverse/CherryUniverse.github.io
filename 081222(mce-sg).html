Introducing Solar Globe & Have a merry Christmas!

 

Another year done, and another year over as we approach new years eve at the end of 2022 what did we accomplish this year and what should we look forward too for the new year?

First lets introduce you all to Solar Globe, The all new Celestial Replacement featuring major changes to the server system running on L-NET and a total refresh of the dying old Celestial Globe service. Introducing new pages, a new layout and style Solar Globe is a clean, simple new service running on an all-new service network!

 

Now what have we accomplished this year? At the start of the year CU entered 2022 on V17 with a new design and a much cleaner everything. We launched the Next Generation project early 2022 designed to replace the aging google engine with a much more advanced web design program but this was a difficult feat to achieve. Since early 2022 to the end of 2022 we worked on bringing a brand new and clean website for the end user, going through 8 major beta revisions and thousands of changes we finally achieved this meanwhile L-NET launched BubbleNET and Celestial Globe was born and died. 2022 has been a very energetic year for CU as we discovered what and who we are while changing and ever developing through the year to today and as we continue to pave a way towards the future of CU opening new services and launching new products and services, 2022 has been a very extreme year for us as a company. We thank you for sticking by us.
