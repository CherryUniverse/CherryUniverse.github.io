Welcome to Cherry City Web

 

The latest version of the Cherry City Web made for Cherry Universe 2x and built on the Experia Engine!

 

CC Web 2x was designed to keep the ability to download and play the classic Cherry City world alive and let users explore and find the classic world that ran from 2019 to 2020!

A Little History on Cherry City!

 

Cherry City launched on the 11th January 2019 and ran until early 2020 when the world was closed down in favour for the new Oaktree Universe.

 

Cherry City introduced the idea of Minecraft Packs and brought advanced technology and code into the game even going as far as to bring operations onto the web bringing Cherry Universe into the picture.

Brought to you by Rebuild Archive!

 

Cherry City Web 2x Series was brought to you by the Rebuild Archive Project, a service dedicated to keeping Cherry Universe history alive and well.

 

Rebuild Archive was founded in 2022 and is the reason users are able to browse older Cherry Universe editions and the reason users can continue to enjoy CU services well past their End of Life.

Download Cherry City!

 

The reason that Rebuild Archive Brought Cherry City Web back was to let users explore and experience the first of the 3 Cherry Universe Minecraft Worlds.


Cherry City is the first of the 3 worlds started in 2019 and was the peak of Minecraft on Cherry Universe playtime hitting 7 players at one time. The world is 2nd smallest of the CU Minecraft worlds having little to no exploration completed although has parts of the world spread across 30,000+ blocks.

 

Use the Download Icon to go through the monetised link for downloading the Cherry City world file.
