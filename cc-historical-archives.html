The Cherry City History Archive

Preserving Cherry City history since 2019

Cherry City

 

Cherry City was formed on the 7th January 2019 as a Survival Minecraft world. This was the case until the survival era was over and the city began.


The first construction of the world began which was the AOFD located on the far south side of the city.

 

From then on, Corey joined the city and began what would be the first of the Cherry Universe series of Minecraft worlds.

 

Following Corey the world began to take off as Liam (The worlds most prominent player) joined the game and Minecraft kicked off!

CC Time-line!

Cherry City's Event's!

11th April 2019

 

On this date while playing with commands disaster struck to Mitchell's house. I accidentally destroyed his house and wiped a hole into the ground. This was the first major devastating loss for Mitchell.

1st June 2019

 

This was the day that the damage caused by Hurricane Liam was revealed, The main damage was Corey's house which was demolished by lightning and fire damage.

9th July 2019

 

On this date America fell apart due to Conagh's absence from the area, the decay system took over and caused the country founded to fall into disrepair. This was the end of the golden American Age.

12th July 2019

 

On this date the official quartz to concrete transition began, and the Cherry City Skyline began its move to a more modern and high quality approach marking the end of the quartz era.

22nd July 2019

 

The Cherry City Railway began construction on this date to connect the world together and make a easier and faster way to get from point A to point B. This was the date the rail-station was constructed.

23rd July 2019

 

Some of the first Cherry City Web services launched beginning with Cherry City which is todays Celestial Universe. Cherry City Web services marked the beginning on the Cherry Online Presence.

24th July 2019

 

Season Village was founded on this date, marking the start of Cherry City's Rapid expansion phase. Sadly Season Village did not make it into Oaktree Universe when CC and Oaktree Merged.

30th July 2019

 

This is the date that Cherry City web digitized the notice board and one of the very early Cherry City Constructions was closed and demolished. It also marked the beginning of Cherry News for expansive coverage.

1st August 2019

 

A Hail Mary of a day, This date marks the milestone transition of Cherry City to the PC World opening great expansion for the future of Minecraft and brought about the possibility for Mods and expansion packs.

1st August 2019

 

Another milestone of the world occurred with the introduction of the General Storage Warehouse and the start of the Cherry City Technological revolution laying the foundations of the Quantum Warehouse.

1st August 2019

 

Later in the same day the first and only Mini-game launched in the city, Spleef. It was a new fun game to play within the world powered by Command blocks which were beginning to become popular.

4th August 2019

 

The second biggest milestone in world history, The first expansion pack entered the world adding vehicles in replacement to some mobs. Players had some complaints but the world continued the roll-out.

5th August 2019

 

The scarlet project was completed restoring Corey’s mansion and finishing the house next door fixing and establishing the rich estate as the rich estate. The project had taken months to complete and was difficult.

6th August 2019

 

Cherry City discontinued the item giveout and began the laid back world management approach still seen today. Mitchell was given the last amount of resources to which he ignored them and let them be lost.

8th August 2019

 

Hurricane Ryan hit the city causing major damage to Liam Westmoorlands property and was regarded as the 2nd worst hurricane to hit the city but set the record for the most lightning struck per minute.

8th August 2019

 

Building materials was closed and the building was destroyed, The closure of the materials centre caused minor supply issues but the city would go on to cope with the supply issues through renovation.

16th August 2019

 

The Multibuilding began work, Replacing liam's house this structure was designed to replace the loss of the classic "Shopping Centre" with a central building for all necessary needs.

17th August 2019

 

Due to a nuclear incident, Conagh's America experienced fallout ending all life within the area, Causing ruins of building's, mass destruction and more within the area. It was the end of America's OK Period.

18th August 2019

 

Both Power ON's new HQ and the Scarlett project finished work and was marked as completed, After months of hard work the Scarlett project featured Corey's old mansion and a new mansion next-door.

20th August 2019

 

Cherry City announces plans to make the first expansion of land in month's due to the rising expansion rate's of the city, land was limited and the expansion brought the city up to the tree level where Corey had cut back to.

28th August 2019

 

The original AOAD Building was rebuilt with new technology and a new design featuring the Starlight roof, This design sparked a new generation of Cherry City Design and innovation with a fresh new AOAD.

28th August 2019

 

The end of Power ON, With the fall of Power ON innovation the company closed its main store to make way for the new Samsung and LG stores, the structure was then knocked down to make way.

29th August 2019

 

The maze tower began construction and was the next generation of Cherry City mini-game featuring maze's and a prize at the end of the tower, Prize could be claimed if you got to the end of that months new maze.

29th August 2019

 

The Cherry City free-line opened, featuring the first subway system in the world, Free to use and connecting areas of the city players could get through the city easier than ever. This proved to be a failure.

1st September 2019

 

Cherry City launches the official history records and details in history became much clearer, this is how dates and events became more accurate and missing historical dates became few.

5th September 2019

 

Cherry City introduced CargoLite, The first cargo only train system connecting "The Farm", "America" and "Cherry City" Together. This began construction first to the US on this date.

29th September 2019

 

The Cherry City free-line opened, featuring the first subway system in the world, Free to use and connecting areas of the city players could get through the city easier than ever. This proved to be a failure.

10th September 2019

 

Salty Village hits the waves, Cherry City introduced the farthest away village ever made at 30,000 blocks in distance. Due to the distance a junction centre was needed in the middle to prevent glitches.

18th September 2019

 

Cherry Cities Final Frontier, Corey was rocketed to the sky's as Cherry City launched the first and only space station into the sky in its short lived space program. Rapidly gaining and loosing attention.

24th September 2019

 

Sata Nuke, the worlds first truly damaging nuclear weapon caused major damage to American Mountains after a test launch went wrong and SataNuke was accidentally invented.

28th September 2019

 

The highway to the Icelandic ocean, Cherry City began construction on the largest project yet, a highway from the city to the far ocean across thousands of blocks of land. This brought jobs and expansion far out.

29th September 2019

 

The largest nuclear weapon to date was launched, Rocking the entire world the first nuke brought so much power that one bomb punched a hole to bedrock and damaged a village over 500 blocks away.

22nd October 2019

 

During the cities Halloween event, The largest bridge in the world finished construction connecting Corey’s build to the main highway. It was a suspension bridge over 100 blocks in height.

26th October 2019

 

The city moved to a more powerful PC, This then followed with the world being reverted to the 26th due to corruption just days later on the 28th, During this time faster speeds and connection was brought in.

1st November 2019

 

The Cherry City bank was knocked down along with the demolition shack and rebuilt with a new design and style, The new bank was made with a better security system and even better looks and design.

1st November 2019

 

In the new Viewing Tower Cherry City opened the End to players who could play-through and kill the dragon for the first time ever. This  brought interest back but during this time interest in CC was dying.

4th November 2019

 

Cherry City introduced the 2nd self made pack and one that has since evolved into the current Universal Winter pack, This was the first time the world saw a texture pack snowfall rather than snow slabs.

7th November 2019

 

Corey Bought America, With the downfall of the area Conagh gave up and left for a new area, Corey bought America for around 25 Million in CC Money to which he did very little with the land than make a house.

21st November 2019

 

Cherry Chat went public, The server opened in a public manner and has been open to the public since that day, Chats were made private and at the same time open featuring the first time Cherry Products were open.

28th November 2019

 

Cherry City had the biggest apocalypse that it has seen and brought a lot of fun and entertainment, This did end up reversing damage done to the world in a split like effect with rolling back the world changes.

6th December 2019

 

War on mobs, Bombs were dropped and damage done as the skeletons invaded, We won but caused damage to new structures and the new Christmas park under construction.

27th December 2019

 

Cherry Radio was forever changed and the discord bot edition brought major changes to the service, People were not much of a fan so changed to a Youtube based music bot during Oaktree Day's.

1st January 2020

 

Cherry City evaluated its performance and released its first book for sale to people, 1 copy was bought by Corey and never read. To this day the book is available to read on the CC Website for free.

2nd January 2020

 

Technicon tech was brought to the first TV ever, LG released a 1x1 CRT style TV bringing moving motion images into the game for the first time ever, Black and white flashing on a single block display.

4th January 2020

 

Car's returned, Bringing back the mildly likes vehicles people took to using them again, but with bug fixes and more to them the Car pack remained lacklustre and low quality, Cherry City stuck with the pack until the end.

5th January 2020

 

Apple In CC Launched the first ever Technicon powered building with a flashing starfront building design. Using the Powervision 2 GPU Developed in early stages by apple & Technicon.

14th January 2020

 

Cherry City celebrates its 1st birthday but has a severe lack of interest by the player-base, with a lack of people and a lack of care Cherry Cities first birthday was quiet and the world remained offline.

15th January 2020

 

Plans and more began to be made but the city was becoming corrupted and the lack of interest in the world began to be too much.

Some buildings were rebuilt but it was too late to be any use.

19th March 2020

 

With one final good goodbye, Cherry City changed its world status from operational to dead and its doors closed to the public, Players moved on to the new Oaktree City and the world was archived for future exploration and reflection.

 

Cherry Cities legacy will always be remembered as it was the world that started Cherry Universe, Brought Web development and even Pack development experience and is the reason that Cherry Universe is what it is today.
