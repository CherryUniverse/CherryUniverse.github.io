Welcome to Newsroom, Welcome to V20

Dive in with me as we introduce you to the all new, all dancing Newsroom on Cherry Universe V20!

 

 

Welcome to Newsroom, Some of you may still see or have notices CNEWS still branded on here. That is because we are still CNEWS you are just viewing the Newsroom which is inside of the CNEWS platform. So what is Newsroom? Why have we changed to it? And why do you care?

 

 

Newsroom is the next generation of the well known and well loved CNEWS, Designed to provide a much cleaner and more intuitive design than the last generation CNEWS platform. Still called CNEWS (Newsroom) is the latest version featuring press release support, slowed down news publishing and an all new page style and archival system meaning all our articles and publishing releases will be right where you remember them. Here in Newsroom.

 

 

Now you might be asking, What was wrong with the last one? There was a large amount of things wrong with the last CNEWS release. With a image focused design we had to make image media for every article no matter how large or small of an article it is, we also had a focus on the fast paced news style which we just cannot do or keep up with. So to reduce work we have brought you Newsroom. A simpler, better focused News first release.

 

Hey though, its not all bad, Press releases and News articles in one place. Articles with the same classic CNEWS style and Press releases with the easier to maintain text style. It is so much easier to manage than the older system.

 

 

So in reality, why should or do you care? Well it depends on the person, If you read a lot of CNEWS or kept up with our articles and news you may have noticed the decline in article publishing speed and more. With this new system those that want to use it can and it will provide users with arguably a better interface and more to read. Its the same CNEWS with a new face!

 

For those that did not or do not read our news, then in reality you might not care all that much. And that is fine too. CNEWS is here if you need it and the new Newsroom will make it easier than ever before in an organised and powerful new system.
